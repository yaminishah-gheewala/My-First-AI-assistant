FAQ

Common questions

Does my data leave my Databricks environment?

No. QueryMind reads your schema metadata to generate SQL, but query execution happens entirely within your Databricks workspace. Your data never transits through our servers.

How accurate is the AI-generated SQL?

We consistently see 93–97% accuracy in production across our customer base. For complex multi-table joins, accuracy improves over time as the AI learns your schema and business glossary.

What Databricks configurations are supported?

QueryMind supports Databricks SQL Warehouses and Unity Catalog. We work with both AWS and Azure-hosted Databricks instances.

Can non-technical users really use this?

Yes—that's the whole point. We've had marketing managers, finance analysts, and executives run their own queries with zero SQL training. The interface is as simple as a chat window.

How long does setup take?

Most teams are running queries within 20 minutes. You'll need your Databricks host URL, a personal access token, and the catalog/schema name—that's it.
